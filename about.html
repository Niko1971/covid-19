COVID-19 UK monitor

This dashboard visualises daily confirmed coronavirus cases and deaths in the United Kingdom. Further information about coronavirus (COVID-19) can be found at: gov.uk/coronavirus

Data sources

Public Health England

  • Total confirmed cases for countries of the UK and total deaths in the UK
  • Time series of daily confirmed cases in the UK
  • Total confirmed cases by Upper Tier Local Authority in England

European Centre for Disease Prevention and Control

  • Geographic distribution of COVID-19 cases worldwide

Blavatnik School of Government, Oxford University

  • Oxford COVID-19 Government Response Tracker (OxCGRT)
